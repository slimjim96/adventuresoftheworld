**Generate all Forest assets in one Ludo.ai session:**

```

1. Far Background (3 assets) - Set seed/style
2. Mid Background (6 assets) - Use same session
3. Near Background (4 assets) - Maintain consistency

Total: 13 assets
Estimated credits: 26-39 (with 2-3 tries each)
Time: 2-3 hours
```

**Why batch?**
- Consistent art style (same AI session)
- Ludo.ai "remembers" your style better
- Easier to compare and ensure visual harmony
- More credit-efficient than spread out
